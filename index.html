About the Photographer

Capturing
moments that
matter

Aiden is a photographer and student at Missouri University of Science and Technology in Rolla, MO. What started as a passion for capturing the energy of competition has grown into a full creative practice spanning sports, automotive, portraits, and the natural world.

With a sharp eye for the decisive moment and an instinct for light, Aiden brings technical discipline and artistic vision to every frame, whether chasing the action on a football field, shooting cars at golden hour, or finding quiet power in a portrait session.

His work has been featured alongside Missouri S&T athletics and continues to grow through his Instagram page @ajbphotographs.
